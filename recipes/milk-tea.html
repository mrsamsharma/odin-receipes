Milk Tea

Chai Tea

Perfect Milk tea with Indian Chai Touch

It's an easy to follow recipe

Ingredients

  • 1/2th Cup of Water
  • 3/4th Cup of Milk
  • Dried Chai Leaves
  • Indian Tea Masala
  • Sugar

Steps

  1. Bring the water to boil
  2. Add in the sugar and Dried tea leaves
  3. Add Tea Masala
  4. Add Milk
  5. Done! Pour into cup! Don't forget to use a strainer!
